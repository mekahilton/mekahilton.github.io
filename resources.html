Rescues and Shelters


Rescues and Shelters that I currently foster for, or have fostered for in the past and would recommend fostering for, or adopting from.



Whiskers: The rescue I am currently fostering/volunteering for. Whiskers specializes in rescuing senior cats, special needs cats, and cats on the euthanasia list for whatever reason, but also takes in some kittens during kitten season.

Humane Society of Northern Utah: HSNU was the first rescue I fostered for, and still do when I need a kitten fix! They specialize in kittens, but also have a sanctuary for adult cats, and they take in some dogs.

Hopes Rescue: Specializes in senior hospice dogs, and dogs of all ages at risk for euthanasia, also takes in some kittens/cats. I fostered my sweet hospice dog Maisy with them.

Animal Care of Davis County: My local shelter with an amazing foster program. They are really close to my house and I love supporting them when I can. Mainly I foster older dogs for them, but will sometimes take in kittens or injured cats when I have room.
