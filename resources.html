Rescues and Shelters

Rescues and Shelters that I currently foster for, or have fostered for in the past and would recommend fostering for, or adopting from.


Whiskers: The rescue I mainly volunteer for. Whiskers specializes in senior cats, special needs cats, and cats on the euthanzasia list, but also takes in some kittens.

Humane Society of Northern Utah: Specializes in kittens, but also takes in adult cats and some dogs.

Hopes Rescue: Specializes in senior dogs, but takes in dogs of all ages at risk for euthanasia and some kittens/cats.

Animal Care of Davis County: My local shelter with an amazing foster program.
